    About Us

    A group of young, enthusiastic changemakers, we are the Youth Wing of Rotary Club of Mumbai Shivaji Park dedicated towards making a positive impact in our community. Over the past 12 years, we have managed to leave our mark through numerous projects that have not only brought joy to those we serve but also helped foster a spirit of collaboration and growth among our members.
