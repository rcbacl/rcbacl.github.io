    About Us

    A group of young, enthusiastic changemakers, we are the Youth Wing of Rotary Club of Mumbai Shivaji Park dedicated towards making a positive impact in our community. Over the past 12 years, we have managed to leave our mark through numerous projects that have not only brought joy to those we serve but also helped foster a spirit of collaboration and growth among our members.

    Our mission extends beyond welfare initiatives; we prioritize the holistic development of our youth. Through various initiatives, we empower our members to reach their professional goals while cultivating leadership skills and social responsibility.

    We invite you to join us in our journey of service, growth, and community transformation!


    What we do

    Seva Mahotsav

    We started the year off with a week-long community service project, where we conducted 10 different activities including Baghban (Visit to an old age home), Annadaan (food donation drive), Stress Management Workshop and many others!

    #ZeroTolerance

    In the light of recent events, we conducted an awareness workshop on sexual violence in collaboration with Majlis in an effort to educate our members on the subject.

    Safarnama

    A beautiful monsoon trek to Kothaligad Fort in Karjat which was equally as rewarding as it was scenic.
